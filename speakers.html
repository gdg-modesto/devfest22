Speaker Bio
Floyd Muñoz (he/him)
Events Coordinator | Bitwise Workforce Training

As the Events Coordinator of Bitwise Workforce Training, Floyd Muñoz manages the events and opportunities for potential students. Floyd works closely in collaboration with leaders of community organizations, recruitment partners, and our internal team of community outreach coordinators to reach as many underserved and underrepresented future students as possible.

Floyd joined Bitwise in April of 2020 and enjoys working closely with communities that serve diverse untapped talent, as well as Bitwise’s city-specific team members, who are able to best understand the dynamics of the communities Bitwise hopes to serve.

Floyd’s passion is to provide opportunities for folks traditionally left out of tech. His passion is derived from his upbringing in a small community in Northern California, Redding, CA., which is surrounded by a minimal opportunity for growth and upward mobility. He has spent much of his life falling through the cracks of the public school system and spent many years in his youth, in and out of juvenile hall and prison. Floyd is self-taught in reading and writing and understands better than anyone, what it means to first, contribute to the success of others and overcome obstacles, show grit and determination, and find gainful employment in tech.
